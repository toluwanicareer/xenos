General Questions

All you need to know about our company and how to get support.

Who are we ?

We are a team of traders and risk analyzers with over 5 or more years of experience in cryptocurrency and trading.

How long does it take for my deposit to be added to my account?

Your account will be updated as quickly as your money is processed and deposited.

Can I lose money?

There is always a risk involved with investing, but our site offers a guaranteed return of main investment in the case of a loss.

How can you guarantee a return on the main investment?

Our website has a separate fund that is traded and cashed out daily. Therefore, in the case of a loss, we will be able to pay the daily percentage and the capital release. We also take a percent of the daily profit and add it to the website capital.

Other Questions

The answers on most common questions are described bellow.

How much capital will the website require?

The website requires 30 percent of the daily income. 20 percent goes to the safety fund and the other 10 percent goes towards the workers and other expenses.

What are pools?

Our website run on a "pool" system. This allows us to keep track of all the funds and keep them secure. We currently have 3 trading pools, 2 for our clients and the 3rd is for our safety fund.

How long do withdrawals typically take?

They depend on the block chain speed but mainly takes around 5-15 (unit).

When do we get paid ?

Payments are sent out daily.

Why is there only 50k accounts that can be made?

This website is in its first stages, meaning we only have enough team members to support 50k clients. Our website will reach stage two within three to four months after the first lunch.

Can you have multiple accounts ?

No. Since we currently have a limit on the number of clients we are accepting, we will have an id check after the registration in order to ensure complete fairness amongst all of our customers.
